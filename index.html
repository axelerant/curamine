Summary

Curamine is an open-source project management SaaS solution for small to mid-sized software companies using agile or other software development life cycle methodologies.

Try It Out

Feel free to try out Curamine on a development system at http://webcuramine.dev.projspace.com/.

What Does Curamine Offer?

  • Team collaboration and asset management
  • Baseline processes with efficient backend administration
  • Client and internal stakeholder reporting
  • Ticketing and task management
  • Milestone management via roadmaps and gantt
  • Elementary resource management system
  • Team communication
  • Time recording management
  • Document work
  • Project assignments and tracking
  • Manage daily, weekly or monthly operations
  • JIT (Just-in-time) accessibility to work updates
